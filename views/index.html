Frequently Asked Questions

STPI stands for semester theory performance index. Second question always arise in mind that what are the marks they use to calculate STPI because in GTU result there are two main columns i.e. Theory grade and Practical grade. Both these columns have three sub columns i.e. ESE, PA and TOTAL. Out of these six columns only ESE marks of Theory columns use to calculate STPI. Marks of PA in theory grade and Marks of practical grade cannot be used to calculate STPI.

To calculate STPI, marks of theory exam that out of 70 is required and that GTU doesn't disclose it. You can see your STPI of both the semester by clicking on the following link.
Link: https://www.gturesults.in/

To get admission in degree total of STPI use that is (STPI of semester 5 + STPI of semester 6) Subjects of only external theory exam (70+ marks). Theory ESE component of GTU result is used to calculate STPI. Marks of mid-semester, internal or practical exam are not counted.
Merit Mark (20) = STPI of Sem 5 out of 10 + STPI of Sem 6 out of 10
